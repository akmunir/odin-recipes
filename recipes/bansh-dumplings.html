Bansh Dumplings Recipe

A popular Mongolian dumpling dish that is served during special occasions. Usually made of mutton or beef.

Ingredients for the dough:

  • 2 1/2 cups flour
  • 1 tps salt
  • 1 cup water
  • oil to fry

Filling:

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 small onion
  • 2 garlic cloves minced
  • salt, red pepper, curry powder, water

Steps:

  1. mix the dough by combing flower and salt. Add enough water to make a smooth dough and knead for several minutes. Rest for 15 minutes
  2. Add beef, onion, garlic, salt, pepper, and curry powder to a bowl, and add water until it is a smooth paste.
  3. oil over medium heat, and divide the dough into 12 small pieces and roll. Add a conservative amount of filling, and then fold over and crimp
  4. flip occasionally until evenly brown, and then enjoy with soy sauce or anything of your choice.
